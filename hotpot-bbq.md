# Hotpot & BBQ

Experience the art of interactive dining. Cook your own selection of premium ingredients at your table.

---

## Hotpot

Traditional Asian hotpot - choose your broth, select your ingredients, and cook them to perfection in the bubbling pot at your table.

### Broth Options
- **Original** - Classic clear broth
- **Spicy Sichuan** - For those who like it hot
- **Tom Yum** - Thai-style sour and spicy
- **Mushroom** - Vegetarian-friendly umami
- **Split Pot** - Two broths in one pot
